Research & Vision

My academic training and research are grounded in applied agricultural economics, with strong emphasis on secondary data systems, climate and carbon assessment, and quantitative economic analysis.
Through Tech Economist, I aim to organise secondary data ecosystems, share applied research thinking, and build clarity around how economic reasoning combines with modern analytical tools.
